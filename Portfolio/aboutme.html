About Me

My name is Nicole Janosi. I’m a junior at the School of Information at the University of Michigan, specializing in User Experience Research and Design. In addition, I am also pursing a supplemental studies in Public Health. I’m passionate about creating digital solutions that will have a large social impact. Through the School of Information, I am developing skills and knowledge to design, build, and evaluate compelling interactive systems and have experience in UX research and design, programming, and graphic design. Currently, I am searching for an internship focusing on UX design and research for Summer 2018.

In my free time, you can find me in the student section, cheering on the Michigan basketball and football teams, volunteering with UNICEF at U-M, and playing with and leading my club soccer team, Victors FC.


On this site, you’ll find my resume, examples of my work, and ways to contact me if you’d like to learn more. Feel free to reach out with any questions!
